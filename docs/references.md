# References

The `references/` folder is part of the repo on purpose.

It documents the research and upstream audits that shaped the template:

- runtime/control-plane ideas from Codex and OpenClaw
- hardened execution ideas from OpenShell
- workflow and event-routing ideas from oh-my-codex and clawhip
- governance ideas from Paperclip
- graph and knowledge ideas from Graphify, QMD, and llm-wiki
- runtime product ideas from Goose and Forge

These files are not copied from upstream products as implementation source.
They are research notes and architecture distillations used to explain why the template includes the capabilities it does.
