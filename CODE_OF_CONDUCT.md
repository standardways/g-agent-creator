# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, direct, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- assume good intent, but be clear
- critique ideas and code without demeaning people
- help newcomers get oriented instead of gatekeeping
- keep discussions technical, constructive, and grounded
- respect privacy and do not post secrets, tokens, or private user data

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or leaking private information
- hostile dogpiling
- intentionally misleading security reports or sabotage

## Maintainer Principle

We care more about competence, honesty, and momentum than performative politeness. Kind and sharp beats soft and vague.
